# Liquid Locked Curve Voting BOND (vBOND)

vBOND is the governance-focused form of BOND and represents a permanent commitment to the protocol.

Users obtain vBOND by converting sBOND through a one-way process. This conversion is irreversible, and the underlying sBOND is permanently locked.

vBOND holders receive the largest share of protocol revenue and are the only participants able to vote on DAO proposals.

vBOND can be traded on secondary markets, allowing users to exit their position at market rates while maintaining long-term alignment with the protocol.

Active governance participants who vote are eligible to receive the highest level of rewards.
