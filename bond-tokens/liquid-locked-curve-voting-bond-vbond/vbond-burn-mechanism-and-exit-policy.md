# vBOND Burn Mechanism & Exit Policy

## Overview

vBOND is designed to align long-term participants with the growth and success of the Curve Bonds DAO. When users convert BOND into vBOND, the underlying BOND is **permanently removed from circulation**, creating a deflationary foundation for the ecosystem.

To maintain stability and protect long-term participants, exiting vBOND positions may incur a **burn tax**, which is governed by the DAO.

***

### 🔥 Permanent Supply Reduction

When BOND is converted into vBOND:

* The original BOND tokens are **burned permanently**
* Total circulating supply is reduced
* Remaining holders benefit from increased scarcity

This mechanism ensures that participation in vBOND directly contributes to strengthening the value of the BOND ecosystem.

***

### 🔄 Exit & Burn Tax

Exiting a vBOND position may incur a **burn tax**, which is applied to the amount being converted back.

* The burn tax is **configurable by the DAO**
* Tokens collected via this mechanism are **permanently burned**
* This creates a continuous deflationary force, especially during periods of high exit activity

***

### 🛡️ Governance Safeguards

To ensure fairness and transparency, the burn tax is controlled under strict governance rules:

* ⏱ **10-day delay** before any change takes effect
* 🗳️ **Multiple layers of approval required**
* 🔒 **Maximum cap of 40%** burn tax

These safeguards ensure that no sudden or unexpected changes can impact users without ample time to react.

***

### ⚖️ Purpose of the Burn Tax

The burn tax is not intended as a penalty, but as a **stability mechanism** for the ecosystem.

It helps to:

* Reduce sudden sell pressure
* Protect long-term participants
* Maintain healthy liquidity conditions
* Convert exit activity into value for remaining holders

***

### 📊 Dynamic Economic Control

The Curve Bonds DAO may adjust the burn tax in response to market conditions, such as:

* Liquidity depth
* Market volatility
* Protocol growth and demand

This allows the system to remain adaptive while still operating within clearly defined governance constraints.

***

### 💡 Alignment of Incentives

The vBOND system creates a powerful alignment between users and the protocol:

* Entering vBOND → reduces supply
* Holding vBOND → earns protocol revenue and incentives
* Exiting vBOND → contributes additional deflation

This ensures that all participant actions, whether entering, holding, or exiting, contribute to the long-term health of the ecosystem.

***

### 🚀 Long-Term Vision

vBOND is designed to be more than a staking mechanism — it is a **core economic engine** of the Curve Bonds DAO.

By combining:

* permanent supply reduction
* revenue distribution
* and adaptive exit controls

the system creates a sustainable and scalable model for long-term value accrual.
