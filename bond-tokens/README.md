# BOND Tokens

The BOND token is the governance and value accrual asset of Curve Bonds DAO.

BOND is used to:

* participate in DAO governance
* help direct treasury strategy
* access a share of protocol-generated revenue

Protocol revenue is derived from yield generated across Curve and Yield Basis strategies. A portion of this revenue is distributed to participants who stake or convert BOND into its governance forms.

To access protocol revenue and governance functionality, BOND must be converted into its staked or voting forms.<br>
