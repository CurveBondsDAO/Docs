# Staked BOND (sBOND)

sBOND is a liquid staking derivative of BOND that provides exposure to protocol revenue while maintaining liquidity.

Users can mint sBOND by staking BOND. Over time, each sBOND represents an increasing amount of underlying BOND as rewards are accrued.

sBOND holders receive a share of protocol revenue and can exit their position at any time by converting back to BOND.

This structure allows users to earn yield while retaining flexibility and liquidity.\
