# PoL Deployment and Allocation

### Deployment Strategy

The majority of PoL is deployed as **single-sided Uniswap liquidity**, activated in **laddered ranges** as BOND price increases.

This approach:

* Deepens liquidity progressively alongside market growth
* Reduces idle capital
* Maximizes capital efficiency

Additionally, most PoL positions operate at **elevated fee tiers**, designed to:

* Maximize treasury revenue
*   Offset potential impermanent loss\
    <br>

    ### BOND Allocation to PoL

    * **20% of total BOND supply (200M BOND)** is permanently reserved for PoL
    * At deployment, **122.55M BOND** has already been allocated
    * The remaining **77.45M BOND** will be deployed via DAO-approved proposals
