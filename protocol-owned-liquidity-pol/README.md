# Protocol-Owned-Liquidity (PoL)

### Protocol-Owned Liquidity (PoL)

Protocol-Owned Liquidity (PoL) is a core pillar of the Curve Bonds DAO economic model, designed to generate sustainable revenue, strengthen token mechanics, and provide system-wide risk protection.<br>

#### **Summary**

Protocol-Owned Liquidity is not just liquidity—it is a **multi-functional economic engine** that:

* Generates sustainable revenue
* Enhances staking yields
* Strengthens market liquidity
* Provides a critical safety backstop

This design ensures that as the Curve Bonds DAO ecosystem grows, its liquidity, stability, and capital efficiency scale alongside it.
