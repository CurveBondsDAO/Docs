# sBOND/vBOND Fee Utilization

**Primary Use: Token Burning**\
Fees generated from sBOND/vBOND PoL positions are primarily used to **burn tokens**, which:

* Reduces circulating supply
* Increases scarcity
* Reinforces the sBOND/vBOND peg

**Secondary Use: Incentives**\
After initial BOND distribution is complete, a portion of PoL-generated fees may be redirected to:

* Incentive programs
* Ecosystem growth initiatives
