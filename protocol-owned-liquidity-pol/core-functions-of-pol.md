# Core Functions of PoL

**1. Revenue Generation**\
BOND PoL is primarily deployed to generate high-yield fee revenue for the Curve Bonds DAO Treasury. By strategically placing liquidity in high-fee environments, the protocol captures consistent cash flow that supports long-term sustainability.

**2. Yield Amplification Mechanism**\
PoL introduces non-yield-bearing assets (CBD and cbdETH) into circulation. This reduces the proportion of yield-bearing supply, effectively increasing the yield earned by staked counterparts (sCBD and scbdETH).\
In simple terms:

* More base assets in circulation →
* Less dilution of yield →
* Higher effective yield for stakers

**3. Risk Mitigation (Last Resort Backstop)**\
PoL positions serve as a final-layer safeguard for the system.\
In extreme scenarios, liquidity positions can be unwound to:

* Cover protocol losses
* Maintain collateralization ratios
* Ensure CBD and cbdETH remain fully backed (>100%)
