# Fixed-Rate Bonds (USDBONDS),(ETHBONDS)

Curve Bonds DAO offers tokenized USD and ETH bonds that provide **fixed-rate returns over a defined period**.

Each bond has a set maturity date at which it can be redeemed for a fixed amount of CBD or cbdETH tokens. This allows users to lock in predictable, reliable returns independent of fluctuations in variable yield markets.

Unlike sCBD and scbdETH which generate variable yield, BONDS are designed to provide stable, known outcomes at maturity.

These products are designed for users who prefer:

* stable, reliable returns
* known outcomes at maturity
* reduced exposure to changing market conditions

Curve Bonds DAO supports bonds with varying maturities, allowing users to select durations that match their preferred investment horizon.

Returns are determined at issuance and may vary based on market conditions and available yield opportunities within the Curve ecosystem.
