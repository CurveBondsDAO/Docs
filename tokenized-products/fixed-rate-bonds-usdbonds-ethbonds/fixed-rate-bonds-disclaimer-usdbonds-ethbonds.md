# Fixed-Rate Bonds Disclaimer (USDBONDS / ETHBONDS)

**Fixed Return Subject to Protocol Solvency**

USDBONDS and ETHBONDS are designed to provide fixed returns at maturity.\
However, these returns are **not guaranteed** and are dependent on the continued solvency of the protocol.

***

### Default Risk

Bond redemption relies on:

* successful strategy performance
* sufficient treasury reserves
* functioning market conditions

In adverse scenarios, the protocol may be unable to meet redemption obligations.

This may result in:

* delayed redemption
* partial repayment
* **total loss of expected value**

***

### Liquidity Risk

Bonds may have limited or no secondary market liquidity.\
Users may be unable to exit positions prior to maturity without significant discount.

***

### Smart Contract and System Risk

As with all protocol components, bonds are subject to:

* smart contract vulnerabilities
* integration risks
* systemic DeFi failures
