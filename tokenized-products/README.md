# Tokenized Products

### Curve Bonds DAO Products

Curve Bonds DAO offers three products built on Curve.&#x20;

***

#### 1. Variable-Yield Stablecoin (sCBD)

A yield-bearing stablecoin backed entirely by crvUSD designed to provide **competitive returns** without requiring active management.

Users deposit into a single asset and gain exposure to yield generated across leading Curve Stable pools and LlamaLend markets.

***

#### 2. Variable-Yield ETH Derivative (scbdETH)

A yield-bearing token soft-pegged to ETH that is designed to provide several times higher returns compared standard ETH Derivatives. \
\
Curve Bond DAO generates yield by using the underlying ETH used to buy scbdETH into a few select incentivized ETH stable pools on Curve.&#x20;

#### 3. Fixed-Rate USD and ETH Bonds

Tokenized USD stablecoin and ETH bonds that can be redeemed at maturity for a fixed return.

These products provide **predictable, stable yield**, similar to fixed-income instruments such as Frax’s FXB series.
