# How scbdETH Generates Yield

scbdETH generates yield by deploying underlying ETH into a few select Incentivized ETH stable pools on Curve.&#x20;

These include:

* wstETH, sfrxETH, EarnETH, msETH, ynETHx, WETH Pairs

**Key characteristics:**

* Focus on **high-liquidity ETH markets**
* Prioritization of **incentivized pools**
* Bluechip ETH assets with a long history of stability.&#x20;

Compared to standard ETH staking:

* Traditional staking → baseline yield (\~2–4%)
* scbdETH → Yields as high as 12% APR.&#x20;

A portion of generated yield is distributed to scbdETH holders, while the remainder supports treasury growth, protocol sustainability and strategy insurance.&#x20;
