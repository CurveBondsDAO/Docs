# Variable-Yield ETH (scbdETH)

Curve Bonds DAO’s ETH product extends the sCBD model into ETH-based yield strategies.

scbdETH is a **yield-bearing ETH derivative** designed to outperform traditional ETH staking by actively deploying capital into higher-yield opportunities across Curve.

Users gain:

* 100% ETH exposure
* Passive yield
* No active management requirements
