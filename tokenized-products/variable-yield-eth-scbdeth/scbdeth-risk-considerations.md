# scbdETH - Risk Considerations

## Risks

\
scbdETH collateral distribution is prioritized into high-quality ETH markets, but still carries risks inherent to DeFi:

* Smart contract risk
* Peg deviation risk

To mitigate these risks:

* Nexus Mutual Insurance covers losses in the case of Smart Contract failure/exploitation.&#x20;
* Strategies focus on blue-chip ETH assets
* Treasury reserves act as a safety layer
* Protocol-owned liquidity provides a final backstop

### Variable Yield; No Performance Guarantee

scbdETH is a variable-yield ETH derivative that derives returns from actively managed strategies within the Curve ecosystem.

Although incentivized ETH stable pools on Curve have historically generated yields in the range of **8–30%+ APR**, such performance is **not guaranteed** and may not continue.

Future yields may:

* decline significantly
* become uncompetitive relative to other ETH derivatives
* vary unpredictably based on market conditions

***

### Peg and Redemption Risk

scbdETH is **soft-pegged** to ETH through market mechanisms and liquidity pools.\
There is **no guarantee** that this peg will hold under all conditions.

Users may experience:

* price deviations from ETH
* slippage during redemption
* temporary or prolonged dislocations
