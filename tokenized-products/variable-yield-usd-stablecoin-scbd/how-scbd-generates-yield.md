# How sCBD Generates Yield

sCBD generates yield by deploying underlying crvUSD into established Curve stable pools and LlamaLend markets.

These strategies focus on **high-liquidity, well-established, Bluechip token markets** to balance yield generation with risk management.

Historically, leading crvUSD pools and lending markets have produced significantly higher returns (higher then 50% APY for periods of time) than baseline staking options such as scrvUSD. sCBD is designed to capture a portion of these higher-yield opportunities in a simplified, passive format.

A share of the yield generated from deployed capital is distributed to sCBD holders, while the remainder is retained by the protocol to support treasury growth and long-term sustainability.
