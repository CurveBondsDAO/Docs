# sCBD - Risk Considerations

#### Risks

sCBD collateral distribution is prioritized into high-quality crvUSD Stable Pools and Illamalend Markets, but still carries risks inherent to DeFi:

* Smart contract risk
* Peg deviation risk
* Bad debt risk

To mitigate these risks:

* Nexus Mutual Insurance covers losses in the case of Smart Contract failure/exploitation.&#x20;
* Strategies focus on High TVL blue-chip Stable Pools and Illamalend Markets&#x20;
* Treasury reserves act as a safety layer
* Protocol-owned liquidity provides a final backstop

### Variable Yield; Stability Not Guaranteed

sCBD is a yield-bearing stablecoin backed by crvUSD-based strategies.

While designed to maintain stability, sCBD is **not guaranteed to maintain a fixed value** under all market conditions.

***

### Yield Variability

sCBD yield depends on:

* Curve pool performance
* LlamaLend markets
* incentive structures

Yields may:

* fluctuate significantly
* decline over time
* underperform alternative stablecoin strategies

***

### Peg and Liquidity Risk

Although CBD is designed to to be soft pegged to crvUSD, and sCBD accrues value over time:

* Peg stability depends on market liquidity
* Redemption value may vary
* Liquidity constraints may impact exits
