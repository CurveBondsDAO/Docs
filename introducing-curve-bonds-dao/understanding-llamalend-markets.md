# Understanding LlamaLend Markets

LlamaLend is a permissionless lending protocol developed by Curve Finance that enables users to borrow assets against collateral in a permissionless and on-chain environment.

In LlamaLend Markets:

* users deposit collateral (such as ETH or other supported assets)
* users can borrow against that collateral, typically in stablecoins
* interest rates are dynamically determined based on supply and demand

These markets are designed to be capital-efficient while maintaining overcollateralization to reduce systemic risk. Yield opportunities arise from:

* interest paid by borrowers
* incentives paid by protocols
* swap fees paid by leveragers&#x20;
