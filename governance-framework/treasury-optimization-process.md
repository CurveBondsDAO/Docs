# Treasury Optimization Process

The Treasury Optimization Process enables efficient and timely management of protocol funds while maintaining strict operational boundaries.

Unlike the DAO Upgrade Process, this system is limited to predefined treasury actions and is designed for rapid execution of routine strategy adjustments.

#### Allowed Actions

This process is restricted to:

* allocating capital across Curve stable pools
* deploying or withdrawing funds from LlamaLend markets
* interacting with approved yield strategies (e.g. Convex, StakeDAO, Beefy)
* collecting and redeploying yield

#### Voting Requirements

Proposals require approval from three independent parties:

* ≥70% approval from sCBD governance participants, with a minimum 20% quorum
* Approval from the Core Team Multisig (3-of-5 signatures)
* Approval from a Certified Risk Manager

#### Risk Management Layer

Certified Risk Managers are approved participants responsible for validating treasury actions and ensuring adherence to protocol risk standards.

Only Certified Risk Managers are authorized to submit proposals through the Treasury Optimization Process.
