# DAO Upgrade Process

The DAO Upgrade Process governs all major protocol changes, including upgrades to contracts, governance structure, and core system parameters.

This process has no restrictions on the types of actions that can be executed and is designed to ensure that significant changes undergo rigorous review.

#### Phase 1 — Community Approval (7 Days)

Proposals must receive approval from two independent voting bodies:

* ≥65% approval from governance participants (vBOND holders), with a minimum 20% quorum
* Approval from at least two verified DAO Contributors

DAO Contributors are active participants who have been approved through a verification process and receive ongoing token emissions based on their contributions.

#### Phase 2 — Security Review (3 Days)

Following successful community approval, proposals enter a 3-day review period.

During this phase, the Core Team Multisig (5 members, 3-of-5 required) retains veto authority to prevent malicious or unsafe proposals from execution.

If no veto is issued, the proposal is executed automatically.

#### Proposal Access

Only verified DAO Contributors may submit proposals through the DAO Upgrade Process.
