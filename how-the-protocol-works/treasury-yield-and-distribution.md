# Treasury Yield and Distribution

Curve Bonds DAO distributes a portion of yield generated from crvUSD and ETH strategies to sCBD holders, scbdETH holders and fixed-rate bond holders. The remaining yield is retained by the protocol as **“extra yield.”**

Extra yield is initially compounded back into crvUSD strategies to strengthen the protocol’s collateral base. Once all circulating CBD, sCBD, cbdETH, and sbdETH tokens in circulation are over 110% collateralized, excess capital may be allocated to additional yield-generating strategies within the Curve and Yield Basis ecosystems, including liquid staking positions.

During the initial phase of the protocol, 100% of extra yield is allocated toward compounding Treasury growth.

As the protocol accumulates surplus collateral beyond what is required to support outstanding positions, new stablecoin supply may be minted and used to acquire and burn BOND tokens or buy and distribute BOND Tokens.

In addition, a portion of BOND tokens are being distributed to early ecosystem participants as vested rewards, aligning long-term incentives with protocol growth.
