# Additional Yield Opportunities

In addition to base yield from sCBD, scbdETH and fixed-rate bonds, users can access additional rewards through optional participation in the Curve Bonds ecosystem.

#### 1. Staking

Users can stake BOND tokens, sCBD, and scbdETH to earn additional protocol incentives.

***

#### 2. Liquidity Provision

Users can provide liquidity in incentivized pools to earn additional rewards, including CRV emissions and trading fees.

This allows participants to enhance returns while supporting protocol liquidity.

***

#### 3. Governance Participation

Users who convert into vBOND and actively participate in governance are eligible to receive the highest level of protocol rewards.

Active participation aligns incentives between governance, treasury management, and long-term protocol growth.
