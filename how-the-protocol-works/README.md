# How the Protocol Works

Curve Bonds DAO operates by routing user capital through a structured system of minting, liquidity management, and yield generation.

#### Step 1 — User Entry

Users acquire CBD, sCBD, cbdETH, or scbdETH through the Curve stable pool or supported interfaces.

This process abstracts away the underlying minting and redemption mechanics, allowing users to interact with the system through simple swaps.

#### Step 2 — Treasury Allocation

When demand for CBD, sCBD, cbdETH, or scbdETH increases, the DAO allocates capital into crvUSD/scrvUSD/ETH/ETH derivative strategies, including Curve stable pools and LlamaLend markets.

These positions serve as both:

* yield-generating strategies
* collateral backing for all circulating CBD and sCBD

#### Step 3 — Yield Generation

Deployed capital generates yield across multiple sources within the Curve and Yield Basis ecosystems.

This includes:

* stable pool trading fees
* lending market interest
* staking rewards from ecosystem assets
* Protocol Incentives

#### Step 4 — Yield Distribution

A portion of generated yield is distributed to sCBD holders, scbdETH holders and fixed-rate bond holders.&#x20;

The remaining yield is retained by the protocol as “extra yield,” which is used to:

* support collateralization levels&#x20;
* grow treasury reserves
* support long-term protocol value

#### Step 5 — Value Accrual

As the treasury grows and generates excess yield, value is directed toward BOND token holders through:

* revenue distribution
* token buy-and-burn mechanisms
* governance incentives

***

This system is designed to provide users with simplified access to advanced yield strategies while maintaining strong collateral backing and long-term sustainability.
