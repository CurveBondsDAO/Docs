# Risks and Security

### Risk and Security

Curve Bonds DAO is designed to balance yield generation with capital preservation through conservative strategy selection, overcollateralization, and layered governance controls.

#### Collateralization Model

All CBD, sCBD, cbdETH, and sdcbETH tokens in circulation are designed to be backed by positions in crvUSD and scrvUSD strategies, including Curve stable pools and LlamaLend markets.

The protocol makes every attempt to maintain a minimum overcollateralization buffer of 10% before allocating capital into non-stable or higher volatility strategies. This ensures that all circulating stable assets remain fully supported under normal operating conditions.

When the treasury diversifies into non-stable strategies, at least 50% of additional yield generated beyond the required collateral buffer is continuously compounded back into stable strategies to reinforce system stability over time.

#### Strategy Risk

Yield is generated by deploying capital into Curve stable pools, LlamaLend markets, and approved staking strategies. While these systems are established within the ecosystem, they are subject to risks including:

* smart contract vulnerabilities
* liquidity fluctuations
* bad debt

The DAO prioritizes high-liquidity, well-established markets to reduce exposure to these risks.

#### Treasury Risk Management

In the event of losses within deployed strategies, the DAO is designed to rebalance treasury positions and PoL to maintain full collateralization of all circulating CBD and sCBD tokens.

This may include reducing or unwinding positions in non-stable assets in order to preserve the integrity of the protocol’s stablecoin backing.

#### Smart Contract Risk

All protocol functionality relies on smart contracts, which may contain unforeseen vulnerabilities. Governance and multisig review processes are designed to mitigate this risk, but it cannot be fully eliminated.

#### Governance Risk

Protocol decisions are controlled by token holders and governance participants. While this enables decentralization, it introduces the possibility of:

* governance attacks
* misaligned incentives
* poor decision-making

The multi-layer governance model, including contributor validation, weighted voting and multisig protection, is designed to reduce these risks as much as possible.&#x20;

#### Stablecoin Risk

CBD and sCBD are built on crvUSD and are influenced by its stability mechanisms. While designed to maintain stability, deviations from peg may occur briefly under extreme market conditions.

***

Curve Bonds DAO continuously evaluates and adjusts its strategies, collateralization policies, and governance processes to improve security and long-term sustainability.
