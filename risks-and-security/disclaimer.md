# Disclaimer

### No Guarantees; Risk of Total Loss

Curve Bonds DAO is a decentralized finance (“DeFi”) protocol that deploys user-supplied capital into third-party smart contracts, liquidity pools, and lending systems. Participation in the protocol involves **substantial risk**, including the **possible loss of all deposited funds.**

While the protocol incorporates multiple layers of risk mitigation—including insurance, loss reimbursement pools, risk management, active treasury management, diversified strategies,—**these mechanisms are not guarantees of safety or capital preservation**.

In extreme market conditions, including but not limited to:

* bad debt
* smart contract failures
* oracle failures
* token depegging
* systemic DeFi market breakdowns

a **cascading failure across integrated strategies may occur**, which could result in **partial or total loss of user funds**.
